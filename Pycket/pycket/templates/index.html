Welcome to Pycket

Pycket is a ticketing and support system built using the powers of Python and Flask to help you do your job effectively.

Pycket comes packed with plenty of useful features.

Login Get Started

Assign Computers to Users or Clients

Pycket provides a user friendly, intuitive approach for managing and assigning computer systems to users and clients across an organizations locations.

Locations can be added, edited, and removed. There are tools for the mass migration of assigned computer systems that allow for easy reorganization of company resources.

Create and Manage Tickets

Users and clients have the power to create tickets for any problems they may come across while working with an organizations computer systems.

Tickets are assignable, searchable, and manageable thanks to Pycket, allowing users to manage specific issues found across an organization.
